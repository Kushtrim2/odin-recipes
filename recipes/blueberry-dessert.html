Blueberry Dessert

Blueberry pie is readily made because it does not require pitting or peeling of fruit.

It usually has a top and bottom crust. The top crust can be circular, but the pie can also have a crumble crust or no top crust.

Blueberry pies are often eaten in the summertime when blueberries are in season in the Northern hemisphere.

Ingredients

  • 1 refrigerated crust for a 9-inch pie
  • 2 ½ cups fresh blueberries
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 2 ½ cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Press pie crust dough into a pie pan. Poke small holes into dough with a fork.

  2. in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 15 minutes. Cool to room temperature.

  3. cream cheese in a small microwave-safe bowl; heat in microwave on High to soften, about 20 seconds.

  4. softened cream cheese in the bottom of cooled crust.

  5. 2 1/2 cups blueberries over the cream cheese.

  6. cornstarch and cold water in a small bowl until dissolved.

  7. cornstarch mixture, 2 1/2 cups blueberries, sugar, and lemon zest in a saucepan over medium heat; simmer until thickened, 5 to 10 minutes.

  8. thickened blueberry mixture over the raw blueberries in the pie crust and spread evenly.

  9. Cool pie in refrigerator until set, at least 1 hour.
